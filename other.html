Other activities

Teaching

As lecturer, Peter is involved in a wide range of courses as listed below

  • He is part of a team that runs a module on Scientific Geocomputing. Taught subjects include: General and geo-computing with python, Database Management using SQL and web development (CSS, HTML, JavaScript).
  • Peter is involved the teaching activities of a cours on Spatial Analysis and Systems Modelling. The subjects covered include: Spatial Analysis, Systems Modelling, Agent based Modelling (using NetLogo).
  • Peter is coordinates, teachees and develops material for vocational courses. These courses include: (i) Geoweb App Development using open source tools; (ii) Principles of visualisation; (iii) and Geoinformatics
  • He coordinates activities that focus on Academic Skills for postgraduate and undergraduate students
